Who Are We?

Team Overclocked is a dedicated group of script developers, moderators, and testers focused on creating, testing, and sharing high-quality scripts. Our team is passionate about helping new scripters learn and improve their coding skills.

We specialize in Lua scripting and have experience with various platforms and games. Our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible with scripting while maintaining a strong community of developers and users.

With team members skilled in different aspects of development, testing, and moderation, we provide comprehensive solutions for all your scripting needs.
